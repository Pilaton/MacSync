## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of MacSync seriously. If you have found a security vulnerability in MacSync, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

To report a vulnerability, please email <dev@pilaton.com> instead of opening a public issue. We will do our best to respond to your report within 48 hours.

Please include the following details in your report:

- A description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Affected versions of MacSync.
- Any potential mitigations or workarounds.

We appreciate your efforts to improve the security of MacSync.
